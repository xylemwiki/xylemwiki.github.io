Xylem Wiki

Xylem games is a Dominion group who created the expansion Abyss. Their other projects included Oasis, Rome, and Legends. Abyss is the 16th expansion to Dominion. Rising Sun is the most recent expansion and is the 17th. Xylem games follows a modified numbering scheme, because Abyss released before Rising Sun.

Xylem is composed of 4 people who all create expansions to Dominion. These four people being, Sean C., Liam G., Weston S., and Chris M. Chris was the latest member making his first full contributions for Dominion Legends. The other 3 created Abyss, and Oasis together, and Rome was made by Weston S. To see who created each card check... For Legends, Abyss, Oasis: the Bottom Right, For Rome, Alchemy 2E, or Removed Cards: the Bottom Right on most cards or the Bottom Left.

Sean C.

Sean C. has worked on each of the expansions made by Xylem, and was the founder of Xylem Games. Sean began creating custom board games with some of his many projects including Tops TCG which eventually became MLB Combat. Sean was the creator of Marine cards from Abyss and led the sets development.

Sean primarily serves as the main card checks and balances editor, and does also create many of the cards. He primarily served as a creative lead for Abyss, but played a slightly smaller role in Legends.

Liam G.

Liam is the founder of Legends and was the creative lead for that set. Liam has contributed to every Xylem set expect for Rome. Liam was the brains behind Conquer cards and the Dragons. His original development name for Legends was Magic Dominion with the subtext of "make magic."

Liam created Wealth of the Sea a Treasure card from Abyss and was one of his first contributions to Xylem. Most cards created by Liam pass through testing and fixing by Sean before being included due to the fact that many cards Liam creates are broken. See King of Dragons from Legends.

Weston S.

Weston is primarily a coder focused on Svelte and HTMl and is responsible for the whole Xylem Wiki. Weston has contributed to every Xylem set and was the creator of Rome, and Oasis both of which were discontinued.

Weston created the slight majority of cards in Dominion Legends and was responsible for the creation of the Instant class of cards which was a spiritual predecessor to the modern Shadow cards found in Dominion Rising Sun. He also created the box art for Legends.

Chris M.

Chris was the last member to join the team making his first full contributions in 3 of the cards from Legends. Chris acted previous to that as an idea giver rather than actually making cards.

Chris also unmeaning mentioned the idea of Set - Aside Coffers which was a slip up in rhetoric stemming from a limited understanding of the functionality of Coffers. Chris favors the Adventures and Nocturne sets.
